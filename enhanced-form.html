Differences Between fruits and Vegetables

Fruits Vegetables
Fruits develop from the flower of a plant and contain seeds Are the other edible parts of a plant
Usually contains seeds Typically does not contain seeds
Often sweet or sour in flavor,eaten raw or in deserts Usually savory or bitter ,and more often cooked
Used in deserts ,smoothies and jams Common in soup,stews,stir-fries and salads
Aids in seed dispersal and plant reproduction part of the plant that supports growth

Examples of fruits

  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Cherry
  • Orange
  • Mango

Examples of vegetables

  • Carrot
  • Broccoli
  • Spinach
  • Potato
  • Cauliflower
